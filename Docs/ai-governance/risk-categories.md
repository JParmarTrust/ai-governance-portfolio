# Risk Categories & Examples

## Overview

This document outlines key risk categories in AI Governance.

## Risk Categories

- **Security** - Credential theft, unauthorized access
- **Privacy** - PII exposure, data leakage
- **Compliance** - Regulatory violations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- **Operational** - Workflow failures, system downtime
- **AI-Specific** - Hallucinations, bias, model drift
- **Third-Party** - Vendor outages, supply chain risks
